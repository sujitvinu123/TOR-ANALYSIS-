- Scans for malicious Tor exit nodes
- Detects potential traffic analysis attacks
- Identifies DDoS patterns on the network
- Monitors for compromised Tor bridges

### 4. **Blockchain Evidence Chain**
- Logs all findings to an immutable blockchain
- Creates forensic evidence of detected threats
